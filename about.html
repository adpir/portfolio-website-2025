Who I Am?

I'm a true creative at heart , passionately blending art and functionality both on and off the screen. With a Bachelor's degree in Theater, my roots are in storytelling, emotional connection, and visual expression—all of which deeply inform my work today as a designer and developer.

In 2021, I earned a certification in Full-Stack Software Engineering, and in 2025, I completed a UI/UX Design course specializing in front-end development. While coding gave me the tools to build, the design gave me the voice to shape meaningful experiences. This combination allows me to approach projects holistically, balancing structure with emotion and function with beauty.

What makes me unique is how my background combines technical problem solving, user empathy, and artistic vision. I've led teams, managed content, delivered high-quality support, and optimized processes across digital platforms—all while keeping the user at the center. Whether sketching wireframes, writing clean front-end code, or designing a user flow, I bring an intuitive understanding of storytelling and experience design. I'm currently seeking opportunities as a UX/UI Designer or Front-End UI Engineer, where I can merge my creative instincts with my technical skills to build human-centered, visually compelling digital products.

Outside of tech, I'm still a performer and maker—an actor who loves creative makeup, prop design, and hands-on crafting. As a makeup artist, I consider myself a visual & digital artist, translating ideas into color, light, and texture. My cultural roots in Puerto Rico and passion for expression continue to shape my perspective as a thoughtful, empathetic designer.
