Design a location-based platform that connects actors with casting directors, simplifying the discovery and networking process for local productions.

The Challenge

SL8 was inspired by personal experience and validated through interviews with actors and casting professionals. Research uncovered a heavy reliance on unreliable platforms like social media, with limited support for location-based casting. A competitive analysis revealed critical gaps — low trust, poor filtering, and vague project listings — emphasizing the need for a centralized, reliable platform tailored to discovering and connecting with local talent.

The Solution

The solution is to create an inclusive platform that connects casting professionals with local actors. SL8 streamlines talent discovery and verification, allowing actors to showcase their skills while enabling casting directors to find talent based on location, experience, and demographics through an easy-to-use swipe interface.

The Desing Process

Research

User Research

Interviews were conducted with actors from diverse ethnic backgrounds and varying age groups to understand their current methods and challenges in finding auditions. Participants commonly relied on social media platforms to discover opportunities and showcase their talents. The research explored their audition-finding habits, engagement with social platforms, key challenges faced, and desired features in an actor-focused social app. Insights highlighted the need for streamlined audition discovery, improved networking capabilities, and functionalities tailored specifically to actors' professional needs.

"What challenges do you face when casting?"
"Finding diverse talent can be challenging. Often, casting resources lack specificity and clarity." Erin R. Casting Director

Quantitative Analysis

I conducted several interviews to understand user behaviors and preferences. User interviews revealed consistent patterns in needs and challenges. The data helped validate design decisions and prioritize features that support seamless connections between actors and casting directors.
