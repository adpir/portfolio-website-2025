Design a location-based platform that connects actors with casting directors, simplifying the discovery and networking process for local productions.

"Looking for your next star? Discover talented actors nearby and cast locally — where great stories begin close to home."

The Challenge

SL8 was inspired by personal experience and validated through interviews with actors and casting professionals. Research uncovered a heavy reliance on unreliable platforms like social media, with limited support for location-based casting. A competitive analysis revealed critical gaps — low trust, poor filtering, and vague project listings — emphasizing the need for a centralized, reliable platform tailored to discovering and connecting with local talent.

The Solution

The solution is to create an inclusive platform that connects casting professionals with local actors. SL8 streamlines talent discovery and verification, allowing actors to showcase their skills while enabling casting directors to find talent based on location, experience, and demographics through an easy-to-use swipe interface.
