Design a location-based platform that connects actors with casting directors, simplifying the discovery and networking process for local productions.

The Challenge

SL8 was inspired by personal experience and validated through interviews with actors and casting professionals. Research uncovered a heavy reliance on unreliable platforms like social media, with limited support for location-based casting. A competitive analysis revealed critical gaps — low trust, poor filtering, and vague project listings — emphasizing the need for a centralized, reliable platform tailored to discovering and connecting with local talent.

The Solution

The solution is to create an inclusive platform that connects casting professionals with local actors. SL8 streamlines talent discovery and verification, allowing actors to showcase their skills while enabling casting directors to find talent based on location, experience, and demographics through an easy-to-use swipe interface.

User Research

Interviews were conducted with actors from diverse ethnic backgrounds and varying age groups to understand their current methods and challenges in finding auditions. Participants commonly relied on social media platforms to discover opportunities and showcase their talents. The research explored their audition-finding habits, engagement with social platforms, key challenges faced, and desired features in an actor-focused social app. Insights highlighted the need for streamlined audition discovery, improved networking capabilities, and functionalities tailored specifically to actors' professional needs.

Quantitative Analysis

I conducted several interviews to understand user behaviors and preferences. User interviews revealed consistent patterns in needs and challenges. The data helped validate design decisions and prioritize features that support seamless connections between actors and casting directors.

Key Comparative Insights:

  • At 100%, there's a direct connection between users' concerns about trust and their desire for advanced filtering to validate credibility.
  • At 67%, both pain points and features reveal a need for more transparent, detailed project descriptions.
  • At 67%, there's a clear alignment between challenges and features regarding location-based talent discovery, emphasizing its significance.
  • Lastly, at 33%, users' desire for more personalized project matching corresponds with their need for greater flexibility in content sharing and uploads.

Competitive Analysis

To better understand the gaps in the current casting experience, I conducted a competitive analysis of platforms like BeScene and Project Casting. While both aim to connect talent with opportunities, they fall short in several key areas.

  • Poor SEO performance, limiting visibility and reach
  • No location-based recommendations, making local talent discovery difficult
  • Filter functionality is unreliable or ineffective
  • Frequent app crashes and technical issues
  • Cluttered UI and poor layout structure
  • Fonts and text are difficult to read, affecting accessibility

User Personas

After conducting in-depth interviews, I gained insights into the need for an app to streamline the posting of auditions and find diverse local talent. These insights were crucial in shaping the design and functionality of the web app to meet the diverse requirements of the local audition community.

User Flow & Journey Mapping

I created user flows and journey maps for both actors and casting directors, identifying critical actions like browsing talent, submitting to projects, and saving favorites. These helped define the app's structure and ensured a seamless experience for both sides of the platform.

Lo-fidelity Wireframes

Low- and mid-fidelity wireframes were developed to establish layout, interactions, and content hierarchy. I focused on simplifying the interface and prioritizing intuitive actions, like swiping and filtering by location or experience. This evolved into a high-fidelity prototype informed by iterative feedback.

Testing & Iteration

User feedback helped refine the swipe interface, profile cards, and discovery features. I adjusted flows and visuals to better support trust, ease of use, and discoverability, ensuring the final design addressed both user needs and business goals.

Branding & Visual Design
